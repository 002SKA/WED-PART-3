Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Kota?
A Kota is a South African street food consisting of a quarter loaf of bread hollowed out and filled with various ingredients like chips, polony, cheese, and sauces.

Do you deliver?
Yes, we offer delivery services within Tembisa and surrounding areas. See our contact page for details.
